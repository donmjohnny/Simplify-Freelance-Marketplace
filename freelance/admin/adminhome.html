About Us

Simplify is a platform for engineers, created by engineers, designed to make students more flexible and ready to work on real-world organizational projects while earning funds to support themselves during their studies. Both skilled and non-skilled students can benefit: skilled learners can take courses and work on freelance projects, while others can access modern courses and trial projects to gain experience. Enjoy learning here, studying here, earning here, and take a successful step in building your career.
